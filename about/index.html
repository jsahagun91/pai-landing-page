About

pai Labs is dedicated to providing cutting-edge website design and development, comprehensive tech support, and high-quality video content creation. Our mission is to enable businesses to focus on their core operations while we handle their technological and marketing needs.


Founded by Jose Sahagun, pai Labs was inspired by his extensive experience in the family's restaurant business, where he naturally became the 'tech' expert. With a passion for technology and a keen eye on the transition from print to digital media, Jose honed his skills in troubleshooting, website creation, and video content production for social media.


Jose brings a wealth of experience from his background in the restaurant industry, his role as a product specialist at Apple, over a decade of video production expertise, and his credentials, including a Google IT Certification and a Full Stack Web Development Certification from UC Berkeley. At pai Labs, we leverage this diverse expertise to deliver top-notch technology and marketing solutions tailored to the unique needs of each business.
